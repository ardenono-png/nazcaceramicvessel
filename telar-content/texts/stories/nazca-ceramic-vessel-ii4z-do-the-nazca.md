---
title: "Do the Nazca portray any mythologized birds in their art? "
---

The Nazca portrayed mythical bird-like creatures in their art as well. The Horrible Bird is an anthropomorphized condor/hawk often depicted with human legs, large wings, and a powerful beak. It was seen as the most powerful predator in the air. The Horrible Bird was associated with warfare and was frequently depicted eating human body parts or holding onto human heads as trophies. Political leaders would include the Horrible Bird in their art in order to show their military force over enemy tribes and internal adversaries.
