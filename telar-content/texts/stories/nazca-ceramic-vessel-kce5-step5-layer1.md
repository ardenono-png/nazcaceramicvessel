What do different types of birds represent to the Nazca?

Different birds represent different omens to the Nazca. Swallows represented good crops since they arrived at the beginning of the agricultural season. Hummingbirds represented wealth and status since their iridescent feathers were used in the clothing of elites. Sea birds (seen on the included vessel) represented success in fishing, since they alerted fishermen where to cast their lines by diving into the sea. While it is not clear what flamingos specifically represent to the Nazca, we can conjecture that they could have been an omen for status, with vibrant pink feathers potentially used for clothes.
